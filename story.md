# Layout
The layout of the tomb corresponds to the stages of Abel's life.

* Earth - Animations.
 * Abel in his youth.
* Power - Fire, magic.
 * Abel becoming stronger.
* Water - Water.
 * Abel learning about peace.
* Life - Lush forest.
 * Abel working healing magic.
* Insanity - Twisted monstrosities.
 * Abel overcome.
* Death - The undead.
 * Abel dead.
* Abel
